About Our PG

SHINE BOY PG, established in 2026, is a premium paying guest accommodation located in the peaceful Garchuk area of Guwahati, Assam. We provide comfortable and affordable living spaces for students and working professionals who seek a safe, clean, and home-like environment.

Our PG is strategically located near educational institutions, commercial centers, and public transportation, making it convenient for residents to commute to their colleges, universities, or workplaces. We understand the needs of young individuals living away from home and strive to create a supportive community.

At SHINE BOY PG, we believe in providing more than just accommodation. We offer a complete living experience with nutritious food, high-speed internet, 24/7 security, and a comfortable environment that helps residents focus on their studies or work while enjoying a balanced lifestyle.

Rules & Regulations

Clear guidelines about visitor policies, entry timings, maintenance of discipline, and cleanliness standards.

Terms & Conditions

General Rules

  • Visitors are not allowed inside rooms - meetings must be in common areas with prior permission
  • Residents must maintain cleanliness and hygiene in rooms and common areas
  • No loud music, parties, or activities that disturb other residents
  • Strictly no smoking, alcohol, or drugs/gutkha inside PG premises

Rent & Payment

  • Monthly rent must be paid in advance before the 7th of every month
  • Security deposit is refundable when leaving, subject to room condition and settlement of dues
  • Payment modes accepted: Cash, UPI, Bank Transfer

Timings & Discipline

  • Entry time for residents is 9 PM. Late entry requires prior permission
  • Absence from PG (overnight trips) must be informed in advance
  • Misbehavior with staff or other residents will lead to immediate cancellation of stay

Leaving/Vacating PG

  • Two months prior notice (or equivalent rent) required before vacating
  • Security deposit refunded after deducting any dues or damages
  • Residents must hand over keys and PG property in good condition
