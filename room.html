Our Rooms & Pricing

Choose from our comfortable and affordable room options. All rooms come with complete furnishings and essential amenities.

Payment Structure

Total Payable at Admission = Monthly Fee + Admission Fee (₹1,000) + Security Deposit

Our pricing is completely transparent with no hidden charges. The security deposit is fully refundable when you vacate, after adjusting for any dues or damages.

Admission Fee

₹1,000 (one-time, non-refundable fee paid at the time of joining)

Security Deposit

Refundable amount held for the duration of your stay. Refunded when vacating after adjusting dues/damages

Monthly Rent

To be paid on or before 7th of every month. Includes room, electricity, water, Wi-Fi and meals
